Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2025

🔒 Your Privacy at a Glance

Nutrio respects your privacy. We collect minimal data to provide our free health tracking service, we don't sell your personal information, and you control your data.

1. Information We Collect

🔹 Account Information

  • Username and email address (for account creation)
  • Password (encrypted and secure)
  • Profile preferences and settings
  • Account creation date and last login

🔹 Health and Fitness Data

  • Food intake logs and calorie tracking
  • Exercise activities and workout data
  • Weight, height, age, and fitness goals
  • Activity levels and preferences
  • Progress tracking and measurements

🔹 Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and usage patterns
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Performance and analytics data

🔹 Optional Information

  • Profile photos (if you choose to upload)
  • Social connections (if you choose to connect)
  • Feedback and support communications
  • Survey responses (if you participate)

2. How We Use Your Information

🎯 Primary Uses

  • Provide our service: Track your health and fitness data
  • Personalization: Create customized meal plans and workouts
  • Progress tracking: Help you monitor your health journey
  • Account management: Maintain and secure your account
  • Support: Respond to your questions and provide help

🛠️ Secondary Uses

  • App improvement: Analyze usage to enhance features
  • Bug fixes: Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Security: Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Legal compliance: Meet regulatory requirements
  • Research: Aggregate data for health insights (anonymized)

🚫 What We DON'T Do

  • We don't sell your personal data to third parties
  • We don't share your health data without your consent
  • We don't use your data for discriminatory purposes
  • We don't access your data without legitimate reasons

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

🤝 When We May Share Information

  • With your consent: When you explicitly agree to sharing
  • Service providers: Third-party tools that help us operate (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Emergency situations: To prevent harm or protect safety
  • Business transfers: If Nutrio is acquired or merged (with notice)

🔐 How We Protect Shared Data

  • Strict data processing agreements with all partners
  • Minimal data sharing - only what's necessary
  • Regular security audits of third-party services
  • Immediate notification if any data is compromised

4. Data Security

🛡️ How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers: Industry-standard hosting with security monitoring
  • Access controls: Limited employee access on need-to-know basis
  • Regular backups: Secure data backups to prevent loss
  • Security updates: Regular system updates and patches
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection

🔒 Your Security Responsibilities

  • Keep your password strong and confidential
  • Log out when using shared devices
  • Report any suspicious account activity
  • Keep your app updated to the latest version

5. Your Privacy Rights

✅ You Have the Right To:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correct your data: Update or fix any incorrect information
  • Delete your data: Request complete account and data deletion
  • Export your data: Download your data in a portable format
  • Restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Opt-out: Decline optional data collection or marketing

📧 How to Exercise Your Rights

Email us: privacy@nutrio.app

In-app settings: Manage data preferences directly in the app

Response time: We'll respond within 30 days

No cost: Exercising your rights is always free

6. Data Retention

⏰ How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Active accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Deleted after 3 years of inactivity
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Anonymized data: Research data may be kept indefinitely (no personal identifiers)

7. Children's Privacy

👶 Protecting Minors

Nutrio is intended for users 13 years and older.

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • If we discover a child's account, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents can contact us to remove any child's data
  • Users 13-17 should have parental guidance when using health apps

8. International Data Transfers

🌍 Global Service

  • Nutrio may be accessed from anywhere in the world
  • Data may be processed in different countries for service delivery
  • We ensure adequate protection regardless of location
  • International transfers comply with applicable privacy laws

9. Cookies and Tracking

🍪 How We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies: Required for app functionality
  • Performance cookies: Help us improve the app
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings
  • No advertising cookies: We don't track for ads (as a free app)

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

10. Third-Party Services

🔗 External Services We Use

  • Cloud hosting: Secure data storage and app hosting
  • Analytics: Understanding app usage and performance
  • Crash reporting: Identifying and fixing bugs
  • Email service: Sending account and support emails

All third-party services are carefully vetted and must meet our privacy standards.

11. Marketing and Communications

📬 How We Communicate

  • Account emails: Important account and security notifications
  • Product updates: New features and improvements (optional)
  • Health tips: Wellness content and motivation (optional)
  • Support: Responses to your questions and requests

You can opt-out of non-essential emails at any time through app settings or email links.

12. Privacy Policy Changes

📝 Updates to This Policy

  • We may update this privacy policy from time to time
  • Significant changes will be notified via email or app notification
  • The "Last updated" date will always reflect the most recent version
  • Continued use after changes indicates acceptance
  • You can always review the current policy at nutrio.app/privacy
